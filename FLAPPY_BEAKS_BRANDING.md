## Flamingo Customization

### Current Flamingo Design
Uses your custom flamingo image: `Screenshot_2026-05-16_233633.png`

### Flamingo Avatar Feature
When users enter their Twitter handle:
- Bird head is replaced with their Twitter profile picture
- Body remains the fabulous flamingo character
- Creates a personalized gameplay experience

### Fallback Flamingo (No Image)
If avatar loading fails:
- Renders as pure pink (#FF69B4) circle
- White eye for personality
- Maintains game playability
